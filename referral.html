COMMON QUESTIONS

When do I get paid?
£5 moves from "pending" to "confirmed" as soon as your referral pays their first month after the 7-day trial. You can request a PayPal payout from your dashboard at any time (minimum £5). Payout requests are reviewed and processed by our team.
Is there a limit on referrals?
No limit. Refer 1 or 100 — you earn £5 for each one that pays. Power users in darts communities have earned hundreds of pounds.
What if my referral cancels?
Once their first payment goes through, your £5 is confirmed and won't be reversed even if they cancel later.
Do I need to be a DartVoice subscriber to join?
Yes. You need a DartVoice account to get your referral link. The ambassador dashboard is inside your member dashboard.
