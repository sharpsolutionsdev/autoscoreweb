COMMON QUESTIONS

When do I get paid?
£5 is added to your ambassador balance as soon as your referral completes their first successful monthly payment (after their 30-day trial). You can request a PayPal payout from your dashboard at any time, minimum £5.
Is there a limit on referrals?
No limit. Refer 1 or 100 — you earn £5 for each one that pays. Power users in darts communities have earned hundreds of pounds.
What if my referral cancels?
You keep your £5. It's paid on the first successful payment and isn't reversed if they cancel later.
Do I need to be a DartVoice subscriber to join?
Yes. You need a DartVoice account to get your referral link. The ambassador dashboard is inside your member dashboard.
