Step 1 of 5

Install the Chrome Extension

DartVoice needs the Launchpad extension to inject scores into your dartboard app. Install it from the Chrome Web Store and refresh this page.

Open Chrome Web Store

Allow Microphone Access

When your browser asks for mic permission, click Allow. DartVoice listens locally — your audio never leaves your device.

If you accidentally blocked it: click the lock icon in Chrome's address bar → Site Settings → Microphone → Allow.

Choose Your Microphone

In the Settings tab on the left panel, pick the mic you want to use. A headset mic works best to reduce background noise.

Tip: In a noisy pub? Enable "Require Trigger Word" so it only listens when you say "score" first.

Select Scoring Mode

Choose Per-Visit to say your total score (e.g., "sixty"), or Per-Dart to say each dart individually (e.g., "twenty, twenty, twenty").

Tip: The mode toggle is located right next to your microphone button.

You're All Set!

Hit Start Listening and say your score. For example: "score sixty" or "score treble twenty".
