Got Questions?

FAQ

Is this gambling?

No. OcheRaffles runs skill-based prize competitions, which are entirely exempt from the Gambling Act 2005. Every entry requires answering a darts-related question to qualify.

How is the winner chosen?

Once a draw closes (timer hits zero or tickets sell out), our provably-fair randomiser picks a winning ticket number. Results are published on the Past Winners page within 24 hours.

Where does the money go?

80% of all proceeds go directly to the player's chosen charity — currently ADOA. The remaining 20% covers prizes, logistics, and platform costs. Every penny is tracked.

Are the items genuinely signed?

Yes. We work directly with the players and their management. Every item comes with a certificate of authenticity and is photographed during the signing session.

How do I pay?

All payments are processed securely via PayPal. You don't need a PayPal account — card payments are accepted. Promo codes can be applied at checkout for discounts.

What if I win?

Winners are contacted directly via the email used to register within 48 hours of the draw. Prizes are dispatched within 7 working days with tracked delivery. 100% delivery rate to date.
