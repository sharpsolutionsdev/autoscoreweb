FAQ

GOT QUESTIONS?

How does the voice recognition work?

DartVoice uses an offline speech recognition model (Vosk) that runs entirely on your device. No audio leaves your machine — it's fast, private, and works without any internet connection.

What happens after the 7-day trial?

After your trial ends, your subscription begins at £6.99/month. You can cancel anytime from your dashboard — no questions asked, no hidden fees.

Can I use DartVoice on multiple devices?

Your subscription is tied to your account, so you can sign in seamlessly across Windows, Android, or Chrome Extension. Active sessions are limited to one device at a time.

What game modes are supported?

DartVoice supports 501, 301, Cricket, Round the Board, and practice modes. We're always adding new formats based on player feedback.

Do I need internet to play?

No. You only need internet to sign in and verify your subscription. Once authenticated, DartVoice works completely offline — voice recognition included.
