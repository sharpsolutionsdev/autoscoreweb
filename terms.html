Terms & Conditions

By accessing OcheVault and entering any draw, you agree to the following terms. Last updated: March 2026.

OcheVault operates prize competitions, not gambling. All draws are exempt from the Gambling Act 2005.
1

Eligibility

  • You must be aged 18 or over to enter any draw on OcheVault.
  • Entries are open to UK residents only unless otherwise stated on the specific draw page.
  • Employees and directors of OcheVault Ltd., or their immediate family members, are not eligible to enter.
  • By entering a draw, you confirm you meet all eligibility requirements.
2

Entry & Payment

  • Each ticket purchase represents a single entry. Entry fees are clearly displayed on each draw page.
  • Payment is processed securely via Stripe. Card payments and bank transfers are accepted.
  • Entries are confirmed once payment is received and verified. Your tickets will appear in your Vault within 5 minutes.
  • All prices are inclusive of any applicable taxes. Entry fees are non-refundable once a draw has launched.
  • Promo codes, where applicable, are single-use per account and cannot be combined.
3

Skill-Based Competitions

OcheVault operates prize competitions, not lotteries. This distinction is important:

  • All draws incorporate a skill-based element in their entry process as required by UK law.
  • Prize competitions of this nature are entirely exempt from the Gambling Act 2005.
  • No gambling licence is required, and no regulatory restrictions apply to participants.
  • The skill question ensures this service does not constitute a "lottery" under UK law.
4

Draw Mechanics & Fairness

  • Winners are selected using a cryptographically verified random number generator (RNG).
  • Each draw closes either when the timer expires or when the maximum ticket count is reached — whichever comes first.
  • If a draw's maximum entry count is not reached, the draw still proceeds with all participating tickets.
  • Draw results are published on the Past Winners page within 24 hours of close.
  • OcheVault reserves the right to cancel a draw in exceptional circumstances. In such cases, all entry fees will be refunded in full.
5

Prizes

  • All prizes are as described on the relevant draw page. Photographs are for illustrative purposes.
  • Prizes are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash.
  • Winners are contacted via the email address used to register within 48 hours of the draw closing.
  • If a winner cannot be contacted within 14 days, OcheVault reserves the right to select an alternative winner.
  • Physical prizes are dispatched within 5 working days via tracked delivery to a UK address.
  • All memorabilia items include a Certificate of Authenticity unless stated otherwise.
6

Charity & Fund Distribution

  • 20% of all entry fee revenue is donated to OcheVault's designated charity partners (currently the Cure ADOA Foundation). An additional 10% is reinvested into free prize draws for platform users.
  • The remaining 70% covers platform operations: prize sourcing, authentication, shipping, payment processing, and technology costs.
  • OcheVault publishes a live charity total on the homepage. This is updated regularly.
  • OcheVault is not a registered charity. Charitable donations are made directly to partner organisations.

  • Your account is personal to you and must not be shared or transferred.
  • OcheVault reserves the right to suspend or close accounts that violate these terms or engage in fraudulent activity.

9

Privacy Policy

OcheVault Ltd. takes your privacy seriously. This section explains how we collect and use your data.

  • Data we collect: Email address, username, phone number (optional), purchase history, and profile picture (optional).
