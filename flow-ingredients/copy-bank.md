---

## Brand voice rules

- **Direct over clever.** "Stop chalking" beats "Revolutionizing scoring."
- **British pub register.** "Mate", "argument", "pint", "league" — not "users", "synergy", "platform."
- **Numbers do the talking.** Real scores (180, 170, 26, T20) feel native. Generic "improve your game" feels stock.
- **No exclamation marks.** Confidence doesn't shout.
- **Lowercase fine for body, BARLOW CAPS for impact.**

## VOICE / AUDIO DIRECTION NOTES (paste into Flow / VO brief)

- **Accent:** Northern English or estuary preferred. Avoid generic American.
- **Pace:** Slower than you think. Confidence = pause length.
- **Tone:** Knowing, not selling. Like the bloke at the bar who built it.
- **Music:** Sub-bass + sparse percussion. No epic-orchestral. No corporate synth.
- **SFX:** Real dart-thuds, sisal-pierce, light digital UI ticks. NO arcade beeps.
- **Avoid:** "Are you tired of...?" voiceovers, smug startup energy, vocal fry.

---

## LOGO USAGE / RULES (for any composite shot)

- Always against a clear field. 1× logo width of clear space minimum.
- Never stretch, recolor, or overlay gradients on the wordmark.
- On dark: full color (red+white). On light: red+black.
- In motion: hold static for at least 0.6s before cut. No spinning logos. No lens flares ON the logo.

## COMMON MISTAKES TO STRIP FROM PROMPTS

If Flow keeps producing these, add explicit "AVOID" notes to your prompt:

- ❌ Cartoon dartboards / clip-art darts
- ❌ Neon arcade vibes
- ❌ Generic "tech grid" backgrounds
- ❌ Stock "diverse team high-fiving" shots
- ❌ Comic Sans / Bangers-style display fonts
- ❌ Faux-3D dart graphics floating around
- ❌ "Hand reaches into frame holding phone" stock cliché
- ❌ Bouncy 30fps text animation
- ❌ Multiple bright colors competing (we use ONE: brand red)
