## Tips that actually move the needle in Flow

- **Start every prompt with "Cinematic, 24fps, shallow DoF."** It biases the model toward film stock instead of YouTube-tutorial-cam.
- **Specify the lighting direction** ("key from camera-left, rim from behind"). Generic "well-lit" gets you generic output.
- **Name a reference film/brand for mood** in your own head ("Apple iPhone launch ad, 2023 Vision Pro reveal energy") — but don't paste copyrighted brand names into the prompt; describe the *qualities*.
- **Animate type AFTER the camera settles**, not during. This single rule separates amateur from premium.
- **Hold longer than feels natural.** Most AI ads cut too fast. A 2.5s held shot with one micro-movement reads more expensive than four 0.6s cuts.
- **One color accent per shot.** Brand red OR a warm highlight, never both fighting.
- **Keep negative space.** Don't fill the frame. The void IS the brand.
